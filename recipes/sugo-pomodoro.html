Sugo di Pomodoro

Description

This is a basic recipe for authentic Italian tomato sauce flavored with extra-virgin olive oil, garlic and basil

Toss with your favorite pasta or use for pizza, gnocchi and more.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, halved
  • 2 (14 ounce) cans passata (crushed tomatoes)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, torn in half
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a saucepan over low heat. Add onion and garlic.
  2. Cook and stir until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes. Add passata, basil, and salt.
  3. Cover and simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until tomato sauce has thickened, about 20 minutes.
  4. Remove garlic halves before serving.
